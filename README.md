## üí° Project Idea
**Nextory** is an educational and community-driven initiative designed to help young people discover their strengths, explore career paths, and make confident decisions about their futures.

The project addresses major challenges youth face today ‚Äî career confusion, lack of mentorship, and limited access to real-world guidance ‚Äî by building a supportive ecosystem that blends **offline experiences** with **continuous online engagement**.

Nextory focuses on establishing a complete guidance and development journey through:

- Strong brand identity and clear community presence.  
- Mentorship programs with professionals from diverse fields.  
- Career guidance workshops introducing industries and future skills.  
- Talking Circles (safe emotional support spaces).  
- English Clubs and soft skills activities.  
- Bootcamps with mini-projects to apply learning.  
- A growing online community and future digital platform.

Nextory transforms career guidance into an accessible, supportive, and empowering experience for youth across Egypt.
